OUR SERVICES


We have been arranging events of up to 200 guests for over 2 years now. Our range of catering services include staffing,tents, fully-stocked bars,party decor, sound and lightning. We recognize that our clients have different tastes and needs, influenced by the time of day
and whether they feel like healthy menu items, traditional food, comfort food or the most affordable options.

Our options provide clients with balance thats right for them. The ingredients we buy meet sustainable and responsible sourcing standards. We have the best management assigned to different categories. For decor, we bring you the theme you asked for and make the best out of it. Our decor equipment is available for rental if you prefer to do it by yourself.

Service staff: We collaborate with a leading staff company that guarantees punctual, well groomed and professional stuff.
Tents: We can offer various options like Nomadic, free standing or attached to your building.
All options are possible as well as in your preference of colour plus with options of adding heating and specialised lightning.
Bars: We consult you regarding your preference of drinks that you want, our bars are stocked accordingly. Our cocktail bars are spectular and firm favourite.
They are ran by a professional persoonel with skills to serve any amount of guests.
Decor Classy, contemporary party decor as well as various themed options are available to bring your event/ceremony to life.
Sound and lightning:Ambient and subtle or big perfomance- the choice is yours and we are there to guide you.
